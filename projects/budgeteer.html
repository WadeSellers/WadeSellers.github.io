Budgeteer

Audio App Finance

Budgeteer is for people who know they should track their spending but find every budgeting app inexplicably annoying. Instead of forms and categories and color-coded pie charts, you just talk to it. Spent twenty bucks on lunch? Say so. Want to know if you can afford dinner? Ask.

Voice-first, stays out of your way, designed for the specific person who is great at earning money and terrible at knowing where it goes.
