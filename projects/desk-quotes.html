Android Always-On Display JavaScript

At some point you have a vertical Android tablet sitting on your desk doing nothing, and the correct response is to turn it into a dedicated, always-on motivational quote display. This is that app. Editorial-minimalist typography, rotating quotes, enough design restraint that it looks intentional rather than accidentally left open.

Built for a specific hardware setup: one vertical tablet, one desk, one person who apparently needed dedicated hardware for inspiration. No regrets.
