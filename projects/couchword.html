CouchWord

Apple TV Puzzle

Nobody asked for a crossword app on Apple TV, but nobody asked for remote work either and here we are. CouchWord brings the whole experience to the big screen — grid, clues, and all — designed for Apple TV from the ground up.

Navigate with your Siri Remote, fill in letters, work through clues at whatever pace you like. It's crosswords the way they should be: horizontal, comfortable, and not on a phone.
