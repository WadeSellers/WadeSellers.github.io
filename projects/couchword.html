CouchWord

Apple TV Puzzle

CouchWord brings the crossword puzzle experience to your living room. Designed for Apple TV, it turns puzzle-solving into something you can do from the couch with friends and family on the big screen.

Navigate the grid with your Siri Remote, fill in clues, and work through puzzles at your own pace. It's crosswords the way they should be — relaxed, comfortable, and right there on your TV.
