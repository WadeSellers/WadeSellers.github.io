AI Usage Meter

macOS Menubar App

AI Usage Meter lives in your macOS menubar and gives you a quick glance at how much AI usage you've consumed or have remaining. No more digging through dashboards or settings pages to figure out where you stand.

Starting with Claude and expanding to other AI services, it pulls your usage data and presents it simply — so you can get back to building without worrying about hitting a wall.
