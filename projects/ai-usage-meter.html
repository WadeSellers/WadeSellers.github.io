AI Usage Meter

macOS Menubar App

At some point you're going to wonder how much of your AI allowance you've burned through this month, and the answer is never where you'd expect. AI Usage Meter puts that number in your menubar so you can stop hunting for it across three different dashboards.

It starts with Claude and expands from there. One glance, actual numbers, no context-switching. Built because checking usage shouldn't be harder than using the thing.
