Matrix Screensaver

macOS Screensaver Swift

Yes, it's a screensaver. Yes, it is 2026 and screensavers are not strictly necessary. Yes, it took real effort to make it screen-accurate to the digital rain from The Matrix (1999) — correct character set, fade behavior, column timing. These are the choices that were made.

Built in Swift as a native macOS screensaver. Installs in System Settings. If you leave your computer idle, you will feel like you are in a movie. That's the feature.
