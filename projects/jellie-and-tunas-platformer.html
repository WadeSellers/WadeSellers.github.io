Jellie & Tuna's 2D Platformer

Game 2D Platformer

Jellie and Tuna are two real cats. They did not volunteer to be in a video game, but here they are anyway. This side-scrolling 2D platformer follows them through levels of jumps, obstacles, and collectibles built in the classic tradition of "this genre was perfect in 1992 and remains perfect now."

A love letter to platform games, starring two cats who expect no credit for this.
