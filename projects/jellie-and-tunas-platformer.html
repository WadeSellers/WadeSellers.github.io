Jellie & Tuna's 2D Platformer

Game 2D Platformer

Jellie and Tuna are two cats on a mission. This side-scrolling 2D platformer follows them through a world of jumps, obstacles, and collectibles — the kind of game that's easy to pick up and hard to put down.

Built with personality and charm, it's a love letter to classic platformers starring two very real (and very opinionated) cats.
