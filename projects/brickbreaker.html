Brickbreaker

Game Classic

Brickbreaker. You know what it is. Paddle at the bottom, ball in the middle, bricks at the top. Arkanoid invented this in 1986 and it still works. Built from scratch because building a game from scratch is how you actually learn to build games.

All the classics: brick layouts, ball physics, paddle control, the brief moment of panic when the ball goes to the bottom corner. Complete, playable, that's the bar.
