Cat Play

iOS Apple TV via AirPlay

Cat Play turns your TV into a toy for your cats. Open the app on your iPhone, AirPlay it to your Apple TV, and flick a ball around the screen. Your cats do the rest.

It's simple, silly, and exactly the kind of thing that makes your cats lose their minds. Designed for maximum cat entertainment with minimum human effort.
